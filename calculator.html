How Zameen Compounding Works

Unlike built-up properties (apartments/villas) which experience structural depreciation over time, **raw land (Zameen) appreciates exponentially**. Land price growth in Gautam Buddha Nagar, particularly along the Yamuna Expressway, is calculated using the Compound Annual Growth Rate (CAGR) formula:

\[Future\ Value = Initial\ Investment \times (1 + CAGR)^{Years}\]

With direct government schemes, upcoming metro links, and multi-national logistics parks locking in capital growth, investing in a gated township like Gaur Yamuna City or airport-adjacent sectors offers a secure shield against inflation with high liquidity.
